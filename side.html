SIDE WALL TRAILER

OVERVIEW

A side wall trailer refers to a non-tipable semi-trailer equipped with a container box featuring side walls and French doors. While many manufacturers produce this type of trailer, our expertise and dedication to quality set our side wall trailers apart from the competition.

At Diamond Motors, we ensure that our side wall trailers meet the same high-quality parameters as our tip trailers. This unwavering commitment to excellence makes our side wall trailers unbeatable in the industry. We prioritize delivering exceptional quality, durability, and performance in every product we manufacture.

FEATURES

  • Our side-walled trailers range from 20 feet to 40 feet in length, catering to various load capacities and requirements.
  • Whether you need a light-duty or heavy-duty solution, we have the right side-wall trailer for you.
  • Our trailers are suitable for 4×2 and 6×4 tractors, accommodating various OEMs such as Tata, Ashok-Leyland, Eicher, Bharat Benz, Mahindra, and more.
  • Additionally, we proudly export these trailers, extending our quality products to customers worldwide.
