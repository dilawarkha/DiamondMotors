FLATBED TRAILER

OVERVIEW

A flat bed trailer, also known as a semi-low bed trailer, is a type of semi-trailer that features a flat loading surface. These trailers are widely used and considered the most common trailers across the country. While many manufacturers produce flatbed trailers, our commitment to quality sets our products apart in the industry.

At Diamond Motors, we ensure that our flatbed trailers meet the same high-quality parameters as our tip trailers. This uncompromising commitment to excellence makes our flatbed trailers unbeatable in the market. We prioritize delivering exceptional quality, durability, and performance in every product we manufacture.

FEATURES

  • Our flatbed trailers range from 20 feet to 40 feet in length, catering to various load capacities and requirements.
  • Whether you need a light-duty or heavy-duty solution, we have the right flatbed trailer for your needs.
  • Our trailers are suitable for 4×2 and 6×4 tractors, accommodating various OEMs such as Tata, Ashok-Leyland, Eicher, Bharat Benz, Mahindra, and more.
